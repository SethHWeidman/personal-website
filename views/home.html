Professional Bio

SentiLink

I worked at SentiLink, for almost six years, from December 2019, when the company was about two-and-a-half years old, to November 2025. The company 100xed in ARR and grew from 15 employees to about 150. Roughly, my role breakdown was:

  • A full stack data scientist, including a lot of machine learning engineering, for the first two years, reporting to the head of Data Science (I was the first hire on the team).
  • A hybrid data scientist/product manager for the next three years, reporting to one of the co-founders, Maxwell Blumenfeld. Here I launched the two most successful TAM-expanding products at SentiLink, the second one of which I architected from the ground up. Blog post coming on this soon!
  • A product manager reporting to the CEO, Naftali Harris, for the last year.

It was an incredibly unique experience to be part of a venture-backed company - I joined about one-third of the way between the a16z-led Series A and the Craft Ventures-led Series B - and do work that was so central that I got to work with and directly observe every function of the company. An incredible learning experience: I became a much better engineer / builder while getting a "startup MBA" x10!
